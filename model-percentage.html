Model A: Percentage

Payments flow through Poly's Compass — 15% service fee on every hour

How It Works

All payments route through Poly's Compass. The family pays Vinny. Vinny pays the caregiver. The 15% service fee is built into the rate the family sees — the caregiver always knows exactly what they earn.

AgencyPoly's Compass
Family pays$35–45/hr$28–32/hr
Caregiver earns$18–22/hr$24–27/hr
Middleman takes40–50%15%

Example: Caregiver rate is $25/hr. Family pays $29/hr. Vinny keeps $4/hr.

Payment Flow

Weekly cycle:

StepWhat Happens
1Caregiver logs hours with family confirmation
2Poly's Compass invoices the family (caregiver rate + 15%)
3Family pays Poly's Compass via Zelle, Venmo, or check
4Poly's Compass pays the caregiver their full rate

Vinny stays in the middle of every transaction. No one can cut him out because he is the payment channel.

Optional: Upfront Placement Fee

Pair the percentage with a $299 placement fee due before the caregiver starts. This provides immediate cash flow and guarantees Vinny earns something even if the match only lasts a few weeks.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Revenue scales with volume — more hours = more money
  • Vinny controls the money flow — can't be cut out
  • Still cheaper than agencies for families
  • Still pays more than agencies for caregivers
  • Simple to explain: "I take 15%"

Cons

  • Revenue fluctuates — if a client cancels a week, Vinny earns less
  • Vinny handles money weekly — bookkeeping overhead
  • Families may push to pay caregiver directly over time
  • Revenue drops if caregiver reduces hours
