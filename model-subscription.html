Model B: Subscription

Monthly memberships for families and caregivers — predictable, guaranteed revenue

The Idea

Instead of taking a cut of every hour, Poly's Compass charges a flat monthly subscription to both sides of the match. The family pays for ongoing care management. The caregiver pays for a steady client pipeline. The caregiver and family handle payments between themselves.

"You're not paying for a caregiver. You're paying for the peace of mind that someone is managing the care."

Why Subscriptions Work

Pros

  • Predictable, guaranteed monthly revenue
  • Revenue doesn't fluctuate with caregiver hours
  • Both sides pay — diversified income
  • Positions Poly's Compass as an ongoing service, not a one-time intro
  • Families stay because of backup coverage and check-ins
  • Caregivers stay because of steady client flow
  • No bookkeeping on hourly payments

Cons

  • Lower revenue per match than percentage model at high hours
  • Families may cancel if they feel they don't need ongoing management
  • Caregivers may resist paying if they already have a client
  • Must deliver ongoing value to justify the monthly charge
  • Harder initial sell — "why pay monthly for a match?"
