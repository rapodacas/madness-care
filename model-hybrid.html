How a Match Plays Out

Day 1: Placement

Family pays $299 placement fee. Vinny introduces the matched caregiver. Care begins.

Month 1: Service Fee Only

Payments flow through Vinny. At $30/hr caregiver rate, 160 hours/month:
Family pays $33/hr → Vinny keeps $3/hr = $480/month
Caregiver subscription: waived (90-day grace period)

Month 2+: Full Hybrid

Family subscription kicks in: $99/mo
Service fee continues: $480/mo
Total from this one match: $579/month

Month 4+: Caregiver Pays Too

After 90 days, caregiver subscription begins: $49/mo
Total from this one match: $628/month
