The Problem

Families in San Diego County pay agencies $35–45/hr for in-home care. Agencies pay caregivers $18–22/hr. That's a 40–50% spread that overcharges families and underpays workers.

Both sides are unhappy. The agency's only real value is the match — and they charge a fortune for it.

The Solution

Poly's Compass matches vetted, experienced caregivers with families who need in-home care — at a fair price for both sides.

Families save money. Caregivers earn more. Everyone wins.

How It Works

  1. We vet caregivers — interviews, background checks, skills assessment. 25 years of hands-on experience means we know what good looks like.
  2. We build relationships with families — referrals from hospitals, senior centers, rehab facilities, and the community.
  3. We make the match — personality, language, physical needs, schedule, location. This is the value. Algorithms can't do what a real person with real experience can.
  4. Caregiver and family contract directly — Poly's Compass is not the employer. Clean, simple, transparent.
  5. Poly's Compass earns a 5% service fee — a fraction of what agencies charge — plus a small monthly subscription.
